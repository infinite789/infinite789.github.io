The problem

“There are a lot of tools that gather a lot of data, but I don’t know any that puts this data together and connects it to each other”​

- Local farmer​

  • Increasingly more data is collected in the agricultural sector​
  • The data is not easy to understand, interpret and act upon​
  • Available tools and applications are dependent on machine vendors

The solution

  • Easy-to-understand tool​
  • Support for multiple machine vendors​
  • Available on mobile devices
  • Integration with climatic data and publicly available agricultural data
We offer

Farmer value

We are developing a personalized dashboard in cooperation with farmers. We belief farmers will benefit from our integrative solution through its support for all their preferred analysis tools in one place.

R&D

Our expertise in data analytics, leveraged with the insights derived from practitioners creates the perfect environment for research and development. We are building our platform to support predictive analysis on a large scale by making use of a cloud computing architecture. This will provide us with opportunities to provide information in the form of actionable insights to farmers, industrial companies and R&D bureaus.

Partnership

As we are all about building an integrative solution that supports any analysis, we are open to collaborations and partnerships with various suppliers of agricultural equipment. Together we can work on challenging projects and build towards a sustainable future.

Customer service

Keeping a close connection to our customers is how we can deliver a product that is truly tailored to his needs. We provide 24/7 customer service and guarantee on-field support within 4 hours during working hours.
